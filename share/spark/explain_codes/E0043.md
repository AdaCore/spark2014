This error is issued when a part of a type or object that is tagged, an
`Unchecked_Union`, or effectively volatile is given relaxed initialization, and
GNATprove cannot determine which of the three restrictions is the one being
violated.

Each of the three restrictions has its own explain code with a detailed
explanation and fix:

- E0044 for parts of tagged types or objects
  (`gnatprove --explain=E0044`);
- E0045 for parts of `Unchecked_Union` types
  (`gnatprove --explain=E0045`);
- E0046 for parts of effectively volatile types or objects
  (`gnatprove --explain=E0046`).

Identify which of the three restrictions applies to your code and consult the
corresponding explanation.
