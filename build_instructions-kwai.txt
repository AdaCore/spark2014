-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
--            HI-LITE BUILD INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADACORE DEVELOPERS              --
--                                                                           --
--                   Reduced setup specifically for kwai                     --
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Do step 1 of build_instructions.txt (create open-do account)
Do step 2 (checkout hi-lite repository)

Do step 3.1 (checkout gnat)
   (if you don't have a copy of gnat already)
   $ svn co svn+ssh://svn.us.adacore.com/Dev/trunk/gnat

   You do NOT need to compile gnat yourself, simply put the nightly compiler in your path:
      $ export PATH=/kwai.a/gnatmail/gcc-45/install-kwai/bin:$PATH


Do step 4 (checkout, build and install gnatcoll)

Do NOT do steps 5 to 8 (yay!)

Instead, do these steps to get the ocaml-compiled tools:

   * Get all the tools from the nightly build
      $ cd hi-lite
      $ cp -r /draa.a/gnatmail/gcc-45/build-draa/hilite/install/ .
   * remove files that will be built locally
      $ rm -rf install/libexec
      $ rm install/bin/gnat2why
      $ rm install/bin/alfa_report
      $ rm install/bin/gnatprove

Do step 9 (reproduced here)

   (in hi-lite repository)
   $ ln -s <gnatdir> gnat_backends/gnat_src
   $ make
   $ export PATH=<hi-lite-dir>/install/bin:$PATH
   $ make stdlib
   $ make install

Skip steps 10 and 11, and do instead:
   $ export PATH=/kwai.a/gnatmail/install/bin:$PATH

Do step 12 to test the setup

Of course, all the "export PATH=" stuff should go into your .bashrc at some point.

To stay up to date:

1) Update gnat checkout (svn up)
2) Update hi-lite checkout (git pull)
3) Redo the step that replaces steps 4 to 8
4) redo the "make" commands of step 9
